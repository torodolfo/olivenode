About

Short Bio

I’m Rodolfo de Oliveira, a global business and supply chain leader with over 15 years of experience across diverse industries. Beyond titles and roles, what truly drives me is a deep passion for conscious business practices and human development. I believe there is always a better way! For me, growth is a constant and never about chasing bigger gains or ranks, but about developing new capabilities in the pursuit of a shared vision. My journey into entrepreneurship was sparked by frustration (and a pint of rebellion) toward the century-old “command and control” ways business is still run – the status quo that stifles innovation and alienates the human spirit. Always resorting on design, technology and culture as a recipe for remarkable achievements, I am passionate about exploring new paths for leadership and team collaboration that honor each person’s dreams and callings as a force for meaningful impact and prosperity. Outside the office, you will often find me starting the day at the gym, practicing meditation, reading and writing, traveling to expand my horizons, or embracing nature. Growth in every form fuels my spirit and my work. This site has been created with the same mindset – a space for communication and collaboration where together we can explore ideas and practices that kindle the inner light needed to shape a brighter, more resilient tomorrow.
